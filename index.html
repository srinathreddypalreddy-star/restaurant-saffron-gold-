Our Story

A Heritage Carried From Hyderabad to Your Table

Saffron & Gold began as a single tandoor oven in a Hyderabad courtyard, tended by three generations of the Rao family. Today, that same oven — rebuilt brick by brick — sits at the heart of our open kitchen, where every biryani, curry and kebab is still made the slow way: hand-ground spice, slow braise, patient char.

Chef Arvind Rao trained under his grandmother before working kitchens across Delhi and Mumbai, and returned to build a menu that honors regional India — from Awadhi kormas to Chettinad heat — without compromise.
